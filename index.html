About

Greetings. I am a devoted tech divinity who was ‘born on the Internet’ - a freelance UX|UI designer, web developer and a former digital marketing entrepreneur. I am the technomancer. My youth was spent immersed in the new technologies in front of me until nerdy computer habits became a clear path to a career. After many years successfully running my own digital marketing company, I developed an affinity for user experience design and the sophisticated way it blends art, culture, science, technology and people. I pivoted my self-made path to begin my formal education at General Assembly where I completed the UXDI program in February 2018 and graduate the WDI program in July 2018 after rekindling my lifelong love of code. I spend my time freelancing, consulting and pursuing my passion projects along the way.
I have two adorable cats and an obsession with video games, pop culture and all things tech. I have been working as a digital technology professional for the past ten years and have an intuitive bond with my work. I truly love what I do and my passion radiates in every project. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions about myself or my services. Thank you!
