About

I am a UI Engineer digital enthusiast who has spent my life immersed in new technologies. Obsessed with computers, video games and geek culture, I’ve spent the past 10 years working as a digital technology professional; a freelance designer, full stack web developer and former digital marketing entrepreneur. I am completely devoted to my craft.

Having worked in front end design and development for years, I decided to formalize my education at General Assembly San Francisco's User Experience Design and Full Stack Web Development Immersive Programs. While I specialize in front end development, I got "bit by the Python" in school and discovered an affinity for server side engineering. I am currently further developing my back end coding skills with classes to achieve my goal of developing video games as a UI Engineer, and until then I spend my time freelancing, consulting, studying and pursuing my passion projects along the way.

In addition to my technological and nerdy pursuits, I am the mother to two adorable cats, my favorite band is Nine Inch Nails and I am always down for karaoke. While located in San Francisco, I have roots in Los Angeles and Ft. Lauderdale and hail originally from Cleveland, where I have remained loyal to all local sports teams.

If you have an inquiry about myself or my services, please do not hesitate to contact me. Thank you!
